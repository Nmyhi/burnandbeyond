"Unleash Laser Precision: Welcome to Burn and Beyond"

At Burn and Beyond we combine several years of laser technology experience with creative design.

Our lasercutting services

Decorative artwork

Intricate and unique laser-cut designs for home decor, wall art, and interior embellishments like: customised wall signs, coasters, corrugated cardboard lampshades.

Business cards

High quality and elegant personalised business card designs with a selection of colours. As special request we provide a design service, so you do not need to pay a third party company for your business card designs.

Personalized gifts

We are offering personalized laser-cut gifts such as custom engravings, nameplates, keyholders and keepsakes. Have you got an idea? Get in touch to disguss it with our design team!

Prototyping

Assisting product development by providing accurate laser-cut prototypes and models for testing and showcasing. We are happy to prototype your own ideas in a friendly cost-rate.
